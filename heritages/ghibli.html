Location


The Ghibli Museum, located in Mitaka, Tokyo, is a whimsical tribute to the beloved animation of Studio Ghibli. Designed by renowned director Hayao Miyazaki, it offers a magical journey into the world of Ghibli's iconic films through exhibits, interactive displays, and exclusive screenings. Visitors can immerse themselves in the enchanting atmosphere of this unique museum, celebrating the artistry and imagination of Studio Ghibli.
